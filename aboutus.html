TKM survey solutions and consultants is an emerging professional and PDI owned company, which envisions procuring the aspirations and goals of those in the professional fields, crafting good solid operational relations with our partners. Our core business is Land Survey with expertise around all types of Land Survey principles. TKM survey solutions and Consultants adheres to Land survey standards and specifications set out in the TMH11
